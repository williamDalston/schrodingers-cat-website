## Content Quality Standards

### Each Article Must Include:
1. **Hook**: Compelling opening that grabs attention
2. **Expert Quotes**: Direct quotes from McQueen transcript
3. **Clear Explanations**: Accessible but substantive
4. **Visual Aids**: Diagrams, animations, or infographics
5. **Real Examples**: Concrete illustrations of abstract concepts
6. **Why It Matters**: Connection to broader themes
7. **Takeaway**: Memorable conclusion or insight
8. **Call to Action**: Related articles, newsletter, or discussion

### Writing Guidelines
- **Tone**: Accessible but authoritative (not dumbed down)
- **Voice**: Engaging, curious, balanced (not pushy about any theory)
- **Technical Level**: College-educated general audience
- **Length**: Strict adherence to word counts (quality over padding)
